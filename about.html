Our Story

About Bounce Brigade

A small, family-run party hire business with a simple goal: the easiest bouncy castle booking in County Marlow.

Bounce Brigade started in 2019 when Jamie Bourke bought a single castle for his daughter's birthday and ended up lending it to half the street. Word spread, the fleet grew, and today we run eight units covering every town and village in County Marlow.

What we care about

Clean kit, on-time delivery and clear prices. Every castle is washed and disinfected after each hire, tested annually, and covered by public liability insurance. When you book, you get a real person on the phone who knows the fleet and your area.

How it works

Tell us your date, your postcode and which unit you fancy. We confirm availability the same day, deliver and set up before your party, and come back to collect when you are done. Delivery, setup and collection are always free within the county.
